Ember Run — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Hi! I'm a solo indie developer and I built Ember Run as a small arcade passion project. This page explains — in plain English — what data the game touches, who else might see it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, email me at emberrun0@gmail.com.

1. What I Collect

On your device only (localStorage): high score, sound preference, death/ad counters used to trigger rewinds, and cached ad files (auto-deleted after 30 minutes or when you go back online). I never see this data — it lives on your phone.

Through Google AdMob (third party): when ads load, AdMob may process your IP address, advertising ID, device type, OS version, and coarse location for ad delivery and fraud prevention. See Google's ad policy.

What I do NOT collect: no accounts, no email, no name, no analytics SDK, no cloud sync, no contact list, no photos.

2. Europe (EEA / UK) — GDPR

Before any personalized ads load on EEA, UK, or Swiss devices, Ember Run shows Google's IAB-certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) pop-up. You can choose:

  • Consent — see personalized ads.
  • Manage options / Reject — non-personalized ads only. The game still works exactly the same.

You can revisit your choice anytime from Settings → Privacy Options in-game. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for ads, legitimate interest for on-device gameplay storage.

Your GDPR rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority. Email emberrun0@gmail.com and I'll respond within 30 days.

3. California — CCPA / CPRA

If you live in California, you have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information (which under CPRA includes cross-context behavioral advertising).

  • Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: open the in-game CMP from Settings → Privacy Options and choose "Reject" / "Opt out." This sends an opt-out signal to AdMob.
  • You can also limit ad tracking at the OS level: iOS → Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android → Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • I do not knowingly sell personal information for money. Sharing for advertising is disclosed above.
  • I will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K / Worldwide)

Ember Run is rated for general audiences but is not directed at children under 13 (under 16 in the EEA). I do not knowingly collect personal data from children. AdMob is configured with the "tag for child-directed treatment" and "users under the age of consent" flags so that, where age is detectable, only non-personalized, child-safe ads are served.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data, email emberrun0@gmail.com and I will delete it immediately.

5. Third-Party Services

6. Data Retention & Security

On-device data stays until you clear app storage or uninstall. Cached ad creatives auto-delete after 30 minutes or the moment your device reconnects to the internet.

7. Changes

If I update this policy I'll bump the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, show a notice in-game on next launch.
